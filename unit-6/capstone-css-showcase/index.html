Pickleball Rules

Serve Rule

The serve must be made underhand. The server must keep be behind the baseline when serving. The serve must be made into the diagonal transition zone.

Return Rule

The return must be made after the ball has bounced once on the transition zone. And the return can go towards to any side of the opponent's court.

Third Shot Rule

The third shot must be made after the ball has bounced once on the transition zone.
